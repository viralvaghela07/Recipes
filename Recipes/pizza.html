Veg Pizza recipe


Happiness is a slice of pizza in each hand!

A pizza that decidedly staggers under an overload of golden corn, exotic black olives, crunchy onions, crisp capsicum, succulent mushrooms, juicyfresh tomatoes and jalapeno - with extra cheese to go all around.

Ingredients:

  • wheat flour 3 cups
  • curd 1 cups
  • oil 2 tbsp
  • salt 2 pinch
  • semi steamed broccoli 1 cups
  • paneer cubes 1 cups
  • jalapeno 6 nos
  • baby tomatoes 10 nos
  • fresh basil 1/2 cups
  • parsley 1/2 cups
  • tomatoes 2 nos
  • carrots 3 nos
  • red chili Powder 1 tbsp
  • turmeric powder 1/8 tbsp
  • red bell pepper 1 nos
  • yellow bell pepper 1 nos
  • green bell pepper 1 nos
  • red chilli flakes 1 tbsp
  • oil 2 tbsp
  • water 1/2 cups

Directions:

Dough

  1. Add wheat flour ,oil,salt and curd to the flour.
  2. Mix it well. Add water and knead the soft dough.
  3. Rest it for 2 hours so it will ferment and become more soft

Pizza sauce

  1. Boil carrot and 2 tomatoes together and cook it with 3 whistle.
  2. Cool it. Bland it in a mixture.
  3. Take a pan add oil. Add the blanded mixture.
  4. Cook it for 3 mins.
  5. Add salt,chilli flakes,red chilli powder,few basil leaves and parsley.
  6. Cook it for 2 more mins.
  7. Keep it aside and let this sauce cool.

Pizza

  1. Make 5 equal size ball of wheat flour dough.
  2. Craft it with hand. Take a fork n poke it into the crafted pizza base.
  3. Now apply pizza sauce and Put vegies on the pizza.
  4. Add paneer cubes,fresh basil leaves, parsley, bell peppers, jalapeno, broccoli.
  5. Add generous amount of cheese.
  6. Heat the tandoor. Bake it for 5 mins .
  7. Do check in between.
