Savor the Tangy Elegance: Red Sauce Pasta!

Treat yourself to the flavors of Italy with our Red Sauce Pasta – a zesty and satisfying dish that brings the essence of Mediterranean cuisine to your table. This pasta masterpiece combines al dente pasta with a vibrant red sauce made from ripe tomatoes, aromatic herbs, and a touch of red pepper flakes for a hint of heat. Whether you're craving a quick weeknight dinner or hosting a dinner party, Red Sauce Pasta is a versatile classic that never disappoints. Garnish it with fresh basil leaves and Parmesan cheese for an extra layer of indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta (of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

Directions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, and red pepper flakes. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes soften.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and season with sugar, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the pasta is heated through.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and, if desired, grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Your delicious Red Sauce Pasta is ready to be enjoyed!
