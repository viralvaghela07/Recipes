Butter Paneer

Indulge in Creamy Bliss: Butter Paneer Masala!

Prepare to tantalize your taste buds with our Butter Paneer Masala – a decadent and creamy Indian delicacy that marries the richness of tender paneer (cottage cheese) with a luscious tomato-cashew gravy. Each bite is a symphony of flavors, where the warmth of aromatic spices meets the cool embrace of fresh cream. Whether you're a spice enthusiast or prefer milder fare, this dish is a culinary masterpiece that promises to satisfy every palate. Serve it with buttery naan or steamed rice, and let this dish transport you to a world of comfort and indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Directions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add the tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. While the masala is cooking, grind the soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste with a little water.
  6. Add the cashew nut paste to the masala and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Now, add the paneer cubes and garam masala. Mix well and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Stir in the fresh cream and let it cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  9. Finish by adding 1 tablespoon of butter and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Your delicious Butter Paneer Masala is ready to be served! Serve it hot with naan or rice.
